### 1. The Line
*   **Protocol:** As soon as you cross the maritime border line:
    *   **Hoist:** Yellow "Q" Flag (Quarantine) on Starboard spreader.
    *   **Hoist:** Greek Courtesy Flag above the Q flag (optional but polite) or immediately after clearance.

### 2. Symi Port of Entry (Gialos)
Symi is an official Port of Entry (Shengen Gate).
*   **Approach:** Enter the main bay (Gialos). Keep clear of high-speed ferries.
*   **Docking:** Proceed to the **North Quay** (near the Clock Tower) for customs clearance. This is the designated area for transit yachts.
*   **Contact:** Call "Symi Port Police" on VHF Ch 12.
