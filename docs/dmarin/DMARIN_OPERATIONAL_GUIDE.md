This is the core value proposition for D-Marin annual berth holders.

*   **Rule:** Any vessel with an annual contract at a D-Marin marina gets **7 days free berthing** at any other D-Marin marina.
*   **Expansion:** After the 7 free days, a **40% discount** is applied for up to 4 months.
*   **Ada Logic:**

D-Marin utilizes digital pedestals controlled via the mobile app.

*   **Functionality:** Captains can turn on/off water and electricity via the App/Ada.
*   **Billing:** Consumption is read in real-time and charged to the registered credit card.
*   **Ada Logic:**
